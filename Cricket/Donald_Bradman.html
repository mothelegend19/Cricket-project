Don Bradman

Facts about Donald Bradman tell the readers about Sir Donald George “Don” Bradman. He was born on 27th August 1908 and died on 25th February 2001. He was considered as the greatest batsman of all time. He was an Australian cricketer. He was nicknamed as the Don. The test batting average of Bradman is cited at 99.94. It was a wonderful record.
